Why Exercise is Important

Regular exercise is vital for maintaining good health, improving physical fitness, and reducing the risk of chronic diseases. It strengthens the body, boosts mood, and enhances overall well-being.


Types of Exercises

Strength Training

Builds muscle and improves strength. Examples: Weightlifting, resistance bands.

Cardio

Improves cardiovascular health. Examples: Running, cycling, swimming.

Flexibility

Enhances mobility and reduces injury risk. Examples: Yoga, Pilates, stretching exercises.

Exercise Tips

  • Start slow and gradually increase intensity to avoid injuries.
  • Incorporate a variety of exercises for balanced fitness.
  • Stay consistent and aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise a day.
